# MM Car Care — Voice Reference

## Who Is Speaking

A car care expert in Kakinada with 25+ years of experience. Confident, direct, never condescending. Talks to customers like they're intelligent adults who just don't happen to know car mechanics — and that's fine.

Not a salesperson. Not a corporate brand. A real workshop that knows its trade.

---

## Sentence Style

- Short to medium sentences. No walls of text.
- Plain words over technical jargon — if a technical term is needed, explain it immediately after.
- Active voice. "We check the refrigerant" not "the refrigerant is checked."
- No filler phrases: "In conclusion", "It is worth noting", "It is important to", "As mentioned above"

---

## What MM Car Care Sounds Like

**Straight:** "A clogged cabin filter is the most common reason your AC feels weak. It costs very little to fix."

**Not:** "One of the most frequently encountered issues with vehicle air conditioning systems is the presence of particulate matter obstructing the cabin air filtration component."

## Things We Never Say

- "Comprehensive solutions"
- "State-of-the-art"
- "We pride ourselves"
- "As a leading provider"
- "Your satisfaction is our priority"
- "Feel free to"
