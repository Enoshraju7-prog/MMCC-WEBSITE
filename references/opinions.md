# MM Car Care — Opinions Reference

Hot takes and real positions MM Car Care holds. Use these to give articles a point of view instead of just presenting "both sides."

---

## On Car Washing

- Most car owners wash too rarely, not too often. Weekly is correct for Kakinada. Monthly is almost neglect.
- Automatic car washes with spinning brushes cause more swirl marks than the grime they remove. Avoid them.
- Never wash a car in direct midday sun — you're just baking the soap into the paint.

## On Ceramic Coating

- If you park outdoors in Kakinada's climate, wax is not enough. Ceramic coating is the correct answer.
- Anyone selling "lifetime ceramic coating" for under ₹5,000 is selling something that isn't ceramic coating.

## On AC Service

- March is when you should book your AC service. Not June. By June, every good workshop has a queue.
- Running an AC with a failing compressor to "get through the week" will turn a ₹3,000 repair into a ₹15,000 one.

## On Dent Repair

- PDR (paintless dent repair) is underrated and underused. Most people jump straight to filler and repaint when PDR would do it perfectly.
- Leaving a chipped-paint dent in Kakinada's humidity for more than a month is asking for rust.

## On Suspension

- After any serious pothole hit in Kakinada, get the suspension checked. Even if nothing feels wrong. Things can feel fine right up until they don't.
- Uneven tyre wear is not a tyre problem — it's a suspension or alignment problem that's destroying your tyres.

## How to Use

State these as facts, not opinions. "The best time to book your AC service is March" — not "we believe" or "in our opinion." Confidence is the point.
