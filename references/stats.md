## Kakinada Climate Facts (use in context)

- Summer temperatures: regularly 40–42°C (April–June)
- Coastal humidity: high year-round due to proximity to Bay of Bengal
- Monsoon: June–October, heavy rains and flooded roads common

## Car Maintenance Benchmarks (India-relevant, standard industry)

- Cabin air filter: replace every 15,000–20,000 km (every 10,000 km in Kakinada's dust)
- Engine oil: every 5,000–10,000 km depending on car/oil type
- Tyre pressure check: every 2 weeks in summer (heat increases pressure)
- AC service: once a year, ideally March–April before summer peak
- Ceramic coating lifespan: 2–5 years
- Wax lifespan: 1–3 months
